About WeatherApp

WeatherApp provides real-time weather updates for your location and any city worldwide. You can view temperature, humidity, wind speed, and forecasts for upcoming days.

How to Use

  • Sign up or log in to access personalized weather data.
  • Enter your city or allow location access for local forecasts.
  • View current conditions and future predictions.
  • Check weather alerts and tips for your area.
